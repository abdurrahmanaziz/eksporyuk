## ğŸ–¼ï¸ Image Guidelines

### Recommended Sizes

**Card (Vertical)**
- Aspect Ratio: 4:3 or 1:1
- Resolution: 800x600px or 800x800px
- Max File Size: 200KB
- Format: JPG, PNG, WebP

**Card Horizontal**
- Aspect Ratio: 1:1
- Resolution: 400x400px
- Max File Size: 100KB
- Format: JPG, PNG

**Card Product**
- Aspect Ratio: 4:3
- Resolution: 800x600px
- Max File Size: 250KB
- Format: JPG, PNG
- Include product in center

### Image Tips
âœ… Use high-quality images
âœ… Compress before upload
âœ… Center subject in frame
âœ… Good lighting
âœ… Clear product visibility
âœ… Consistent style across cards

âŒ Avoid blurry images
âŒ Don't use stock watermarks
