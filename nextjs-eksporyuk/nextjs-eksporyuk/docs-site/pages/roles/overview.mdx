# Ringkasan Sistem Role

Platform EksporYuk menggunakan sistem **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)** yang membagi pengguna menjadi 7 role utama. Setiap role memiliki hak akses dan fitur yang berbeda.

## ğŸ­ 7 Role Platform

### 1. ADMIN
**Pengelola Platform**
- Akses penuh ke seluruh sistem
- Kelola user, produk, kursus, transaksi
- Monitoring dan analytics
- Konfigurasi platform

**Fitur Utama:**
- Dashboard admin lengkap
- User management
- Product & course management
- Financial reports
- Integration settings

### 2. FOUNDER
**Pemilik Platform**
- Revenue share 60% (setelah affiliate + fee 15%)
- Wallet otomatis
- Akses laporan keuangan
- Monitoring performa

### 3. CO_FOUNDER
**Co-Pemilik Platform**
- Revenue share 40% (setelah affiliate + fee 15%)
- Wallet otomatis
- Akses laporan keuangan
- Monitoring performa

### 4. MENTOR
**Pembuat Konten & Kursus**
- Buat dan kelola kursus
- Upload materi pembelajaran
- Kelola siswa
- Earning dari penjualan kursus
- Dashboard analytics

**Fitur Utama:**
- Course creation
- Material upload
- Student management
- Revenue tracking
- Certificate issuance

### 5. AFFILIATE
**Partner Marketing**
- Generate link affiliasi
- Short link custom
- Tracking klik & konversi
- Komisi otomatis
- Dashboard performa

**Fitur Utama:**
- Link generator
- CRM & lead management
- Email broadcast
- Template library
- Commission tracking
- Payout management

### 6. MEMBER_PREMIUM
**Anggota Premium**
- Akses semua kursus
- Akses grup VIP
- Database ekspor lengkap
- Sertifikat
- Priority support

**Fitur Utama:**
- Full course access
- VIP groups
- Export database
- Certificate download
- Community features

### 7. MEMBER_FREE
**Anggota Gratis**
- Akses terbatas
- Feed komunitas
- Event gratis
- Upgrade kapan saja

**Fitur Terbatas:**
- Basic community access
- Free events
- Limited courses

## ğŸ“ Catatan Penting

1. **Satu User, Satu Role** - Setiap user hanya bisa memiliki 1 role aktif
2. **Role Founder/Co-Founder** - Ditentukan oleh system flag (`isFounder`, `isCoFounder`)
3. **Upgrade Otomatis** - Pembayaran membership langsung upgrade role
4. **Apply Affiliate** - Member bisa apply jadi affiliate (perlu approval admin)
5. **Security** - Semua endpoint API terproteksi berdasarkan role

- **Middleware** (`/src/middleware.ts`) - Route protection
- **API Routes** - Session & role validation
- **UI Components** - Conditional rendering
